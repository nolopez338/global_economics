\section{G10\_T1\_L1\_C1-C2\_practice\_activity1}
This activity develops C1--C2 skills by identifying decision components and organizing consequences in payoff tables.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Local Café Investment:} Identify alternatives, states, and payoff entries.
    \item \textbf{Green Energy Production Mix:} Organize strategy, weather, and consequence information.
    \item \textbf{Global Shipping Network Design:} Interpret alternatives and states with positive and negative payoffs.
    \item \textbf{Community Bakery Lunch Boxes:} Build payoffs from quantities and unit profits.
    \item \textbf{Rural Internet Subscriptions:} Classify plans and enrollment states into a payoff table.
    \item \textbf{Coffee Export Routes:} Match route alternatives with exchange-rate states and payoffs.
    \item \textbf{Lake Ferry Tourism:} Compare ferry alternatives across seasonal states.
    \item \textbf{Cold-Storage Contracts:} Organize demand states, unit profits, and fixed profits.
    \item \textbf{Solar Installation Bids:} Interpret policy states and fixed adjustments in payoffs.
    \item \textbf{Air Cargo Charters:} Build lease payoffs from volumes, fuel states, and fixed profits.
    \item \textbf{Seafood Export Licenses:} Organize export alternatives, seasonal states, and adjusted payoffs.
\end{enumerate}
