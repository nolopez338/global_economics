\section{Problem 9 --- Emergency Supply Contract (C1--C2)}
\label{sec:problem9-emergency-supply-contract-c1-c2}
A city signs a contract for first-aid supply kits. Revenue includes a fixed service retainer paid by the city and a variable payment per kit delivered. Costs include a variable cost per kit plus a state-based compliance fee.

The decision alternatives are:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Local supplier}
    \item \textbf{National supplier}
\end{itemize}

The states of nature and probabilities are:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Normal demand $\to 0.50$
    \item Surge demand $\to 0.30$
    \item Emergency demand $\to 0.20$
\end{itemize}

Given information:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Fixed retainer paid by the city (thousand USD): 12 under normal demand, 18 under surge demand, and 28 under emergency demand.
    \item Kits delivered (thousands): Local supplier delivers 6, 9, and 12 under normal, surge, and emergency demand; National supplier delivers 7, 10, and 13.
    \item Revenue per kit (USD): Local 10, National 8.5. Cost per kit (USD): Local 6.2, National 5.0. Fixed compliance fee (thousand USD): 6, 10, and 18 under normal, surge, and emergency demand.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Question:} Interpret the decision alternatives, events, consequences, and states of nature, then build the payoff table.

\textbf{Questions/Tasks.}

    \item \TaskHyperref{subsec:problem9-emergency-supply-contract-c1}{\textbf{(C1)} Interpreting decision alternatives, events, consequences, and states.}
    \item \TaskHyperref{subsec:problem9-emergency-supply-contract-c2}{\textbf{(C2)} Building the payoff table.}
\end{enumerate}
