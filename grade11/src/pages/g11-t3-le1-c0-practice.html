Problem 1 — App Notifications per Hour

Problem. Let \(X\) be the number of app notifications a student receives in one hour. The probability mass function is

x0123
\(P(X=x)\)0.20.3\(k\)0.1

Question. (a) Determine \(k\) so that this is a valid probability distribution. (b) Compute: (i) \(P(X=2)\), (ii) \(P(X\le 2)\), and (iii) \(P(1\le X\le 2)\). (c) Interpret these results in terms of percentage probability.

Problem 7 — Correct Answers on a Short Quiz

Problem 10 — Goals in a Match Summary

Problem. Let \(T\) be the number of goals scored by a team in a match summary. The pmf is

\[P(T=t)=\begin{cases}p,& t=0,\\\frac{3}{4}-2p,& t=1,\\p,& t=2,\\\frac{1}{4},& t=3.\end{cases}\]

Question. (a) Determine all valid values of \(p\). Since all listed probabilities must satisfy nonnegativity, require \(p\ge0\) and \(\frac{3}{4}-2p\ge0\), so \(0\le p\le\frac{3}{8}\). (b) Compute: (i) \(P(T=1)\), (ii) \(P(T\in\{0,2,3\})\), and (iii) \(P(1\le T\le 3)\). (c) Interpret these results in terms of percentage probability.
