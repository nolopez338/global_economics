Evaluated criterion

  • C7: Uses one-sample mean \(z\)-tests with known \(\sigma\) to find the minimum sample size required to reject \(H_0\), then interprets the result in context.

\[z(n)=\frac{\bar x-\mu_0}{\sigma/\sqrt{n}}\]

Reject \(H_0\) when: higher-tail \(z(n)\ge z_\alpha\), lower-tail \(z(n)\le -z_\alpha\), two-tailed \(|z(n)|\ge z_{\alpha/2}\).

  • Problem 3: Two-tailed, one significance level
