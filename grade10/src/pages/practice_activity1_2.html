A food truck is choosing between two menu strategies for selling meals at lunchtime. Revenue comes from meal sales, and costs cover ingredients and staffing for the season.

Two strategies are being compared:
• Expanded menu
• Classic menu

Given information (thousand USD):
Revenue states of nature:
• High demand (0.60)
• Moderate demand (0.40)
• Low demand (0.60)

Cost states of nature:
• Low ingredient cost (0.55)
• High ingredient cost (0.45)

Revenue by demand state:
• High demand: Expanded 36, Classic 30
• Moderate demand: Expanded 28, Classic 26
• Low demand: Expanded 20, Classic 22

Cost by ingredient-cost state:
• Low cost: Expanded 12, Classic 10
• High cost: Expanded 15, Classic 13

Question: Compute profit for each state and decide using Maximin, Expected Value, and Maximax.
