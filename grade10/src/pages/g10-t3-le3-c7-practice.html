Exam scores follow \(X\sim N(68,8^2)\).

↑

\[P(X<x_1)=0.8023,\qquad P(X<x_2)=0.1151.\]

\[\Phi(z)=0.8023\Rightarrow z_1=0.85,\quad \Phi(z)=0.1151\Rightarrow z_2=-1.20.\]

\[Z=\frac{X-68}{8},\qquad x=68+z(8).\]

\[x_1=74.8,\qquad x_2=58.4.\]

↑

Interpretation: About 80.23\% score below 74.8; 11.51\% score below 58.4.
