Let $X\sim \text{Uniform}(0,10)$.

Questions/Tasks.

  • (a) the density value $f(x)$ on the support,
  • (b) the support interval of $X$,
  • (c) $P(2\le X\le 7)$,
  • (d) $P(X>8)$,
  • (e) $P(X\le 8)$.
↑

C2.

Prompt. (a) the density value $f(x)$ on the support,

For $X\sim \text{Uniform}(0,10)$,
$$ f(x)=\frac{1}{10-0}=\frac{1}{10},\qquad 0\le x\le 10.
$$

↑

Prompt. (b) the support interval of $X$,

The support is $[0,10]$.

$$ P(0\le X\le 10)=1.
$$

Support interval $[0,10]$

↑

$$ P(2\le X\le 7)=\frac{7-2}{10}=\frac{1}{2}.
$$

Region for $P(2\le X\le7)$

↑

Prompt. (d) $P(X>8)$,

$$ P(X>8)=\frac{10-8}{10}=\frac{1}{5}.
$$

Region for $P(X>8)$

↑

Prompt. (e) $P(X\le 8)$.

For $P(X\le 8)$, the standard approach gives
$$ P(X\le 8)=\frac{8-0}{10}=\frac{4}{5}.
$$ Using the complement approach,
$$ P(X\le 8)=1-P(X>8)=1-\frac{1}{5}=\frac{4}{5}.
$$ So both approaches give the same value.

Region for $P(X\le8)$
