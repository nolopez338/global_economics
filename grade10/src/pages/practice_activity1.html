Problem 1 — Local Café Investment

A small café is considering whether to introduce a new line of artisan desserts. The owner has two options:

• Launch the dessert line, in which case profits depend on demand:   – High demand → 40 thousand USD   – Low demand → 10 thousand USD
• Keep the current menu, yielding 25 thousand USD regardless of demand.

Market research indicates: • Probability of high demand: 0.60 • Probability of low demand: 0.40

Question: Make a decision on the best option based on three perspectives: safe behavior (Maximin), balanced behavior (Expected Value), and risky behavior (Maximax).

Problem 2 — Green Energy Production Mix

A renewable-energy company is evaluating its energy production strategy for the coming year. Three weather conditions are possible:

• Windy • Sunny • Cloudy

The company is comparing three strategies: 1. Build wind turbines 2. Build solar farms 3. Build a balanced hybrid system

Expected profits (in million USD):
• Wind turbines → 85 (Windy), 40 (Sunny), 25 (Cloudy) • Solar farms → 30 (Windy), 90 (Sunny), 20 (Cloudy) • Hybrid system → 60 (Windy), 60 (Sunny), 55 (Cloudy)

Weather probabilities: • Windy → 0.30 • Sunny → 0.50 • Cloudy → 0.20

Question: Make a decision on the best strategy based on three perspectives: safe behavior (Maximin), balanced behavior (Expected Value), and risky behavior (Maximax).

Problem 3 — Global Shipping Network Design

A multinational logistics firm must redesign its global shipping network for the next five years. The firm is considering three network configurations:

1. Centralized: A single mega-hub in East Asia 2. Regional: Three hubs (Americas, Europe, Asia) 3. Adaptive AI–Driven: Dynamic routing using machine-learning models

Three volatility scenarios are possible:
• Low Volatility — stable trade flows • Medium Volatility — moderate regional disruptions • High Volatility — significant unpredictability

Expected profits (million USD):
• Centralized → 210 (Low), 130 (Medium), –40 (High) • Regional → 180 (Low), 160 (Medium), 70 (High) • AI–Driven → 150 (Low), 175 (Medium), 140 (High)

Scenario probabilities: • Low → 0.25 • Medium → 0.45 • High → 0.30

Question: Make a decision on the best configuration based on three perspectives: safe behavior (Maximin), balanced behavior (Expected Value), and risky behavior (Maximax).

Solution 1 — Local Café Investment

Restated Problem

A small café may launch a new dessert line. Payoffs: • Launch: 40k (high demand), 10k (low demand) • Keep menu: 25k (high demand), 25k (low demand) Probabilities: 0.60 (high), 0.40 (low). Question: Make a decision on the best option for safe behavior (Maximin), balanced behavior (Expected Value), and risky behavior (Maximax).

Payoff Table

AlternativeHigh demandLow demand
Launch desserts4010
Keep menu2525

Expected Value (balanced behavior)

\[ EV_{\text{Launch}} = 0.60(40) + 0.40(10) = 24 + 4 = 28 \] \[ EV_{\text{Keep}} = 0.60(25) + 0.40(25) = 15 + 10 = 25 \] Balanced behavior (Expected Value) recommends: Launch desserts.

Maximin (safe behavior)

Compute the worst payoff for each option: \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Launch}, S_i) = \min\{40, 10\} = 10 \] \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Keep}, S_i) = \min\{25, 25\} = 25 \] The safe behavior (Maximin) chooses the option whose worst payoff is largest: Safe behavior recommends: Keep the current menu.

Maximax (risky behavior)

Compute the best payoff for each option: \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Launch}, S_i) = \max\{40, 10\} = 40 \] \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Keep}, S_i) = \max\{25, 25\} = 25 \] The risky behavior (Maximax) chooses the option with the highest possible payoff: Risky behavior recommends: Launch desserts.

Summary by behavior type

  • Safe behavior (Maximin): Keep the current menu.
  • Balanced behavior (Expected Value): Launch desserts.
  • Risky behavior (Maximax): Launch desserts.

Solution 2 — Green Energy Production Mix

Restated Problem

A renewable-energy firm evaluates three strategies: wind turbines, solar farms, and a hybrid system. Weather states: Windy, Sunny, Cloudy. Profits (million USD): • Wind turbines → 85 (Windy), 40 (Sunny), 25 (Cloudy) • Solar farms → 30 (Windy), 90 (Sunny), 20 (Cloudy) • Hybrid system → 60 (Windy), 60 (Sunny), 55 (Cloudy) Probabilities: 0.30 (Windy), 0.50 (Sunny), 0.20 (Cloudy). Question: Make a decision on the best strategy for safe behavior (Maximin), balanced behavior (Expected Value), and risky behavior (Maximax).

Payoff Table

StrategyWindySunnyCloudy
Wind turbines854025
Solar farms309020
Hybrid system606055

Expected Value (balanced behavior)

\[ EV_{\text{Wind}} = 0.30(85) + 0.50(40) + 0.20(25) = 25.5 + 20 + 5 = 50.5 \] \[ EV_{\text{Solar}} = 0.30(30) + 0.50(90) + 0.20(20) = 9 + 45 + 4 = 58 \] \[ EV_{\text{Hybrid}} = 0.30(60) + 0.50(60) + 0.20(55) = 18 + 30 + 11 = 59 \] The largest EV is 59, so balanced behavior recommends: Hybrid system.

Maximin (safe behavior)

Worst payoff for each strategy: \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Wind}, S_i) = \min\{85, 40, 25\} = 25 \] \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Solar}, S_i) = \min\{30, 90, 20\} = 20 \] \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Hybrid}, S_i) = \min\{60, 60, 55\} = 55 \] The safe behavior chooses the option with the highest worst payoff: 55 (Hybrid). Safe behavior recommends: Hybrid system.

Maximax (risky behavior)

Best payoff for each strategy: \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Wind}, S_i) = \max\{85, 40, 25\} = 85 \] \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Solar}, S_i) = \max\{30, 90, 20\} = 90 \] \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Hybrid}, S_i) = \max\{60, 60, 55\} = 60 \] The risky behavior chooses the largest best payoff: 90 (Solar). Risky behavior recommends: Solar farms.

Summary by behavior type

  • Safe behavior (Maximin): Hybrid system.
  • Balanced behavior (Expected Value): Hybrid system.
  • Risky behavior (Maximax): Solar farms.

Solution 3 — Global Shipping Network Design

Restated Problem

A logistics firm must select between centralized, regional, and AI–driven shipping networks. Volatility states: Low, Medium, High. Profits (million USD): • Centralized → 210 (Low), 130 (Medium), −40 (High) • Regional → 180 (Low), 160 (Medium), 70 (High) • AI–Driven → 150 (Low), 175 (Medium), 140 (High) Probabilities: 0.25 (Low), 0.45 (Medium), 0.30 (High). Question: Make a decision on the best configuration for safe behavior (Maximin), balanced behavior (Expected Value), and risky behavior (Maximax).

Payoff Table

ConfigurationLow volatilityMedium volatilityHigh volatility
Centralized210130-40
Regional18016070
AI–Driven150175140

Expected Value (balanced behavior)

\[ EV_{\text{Centralized}} = 0.25(210) + 0.45(130) + 0.30(-40) = 52.5 + 58.5 - 12 = 99 \] \[ EV_{\text{Regional}} = 0.25(180) + 0.45(160) + 0.30(70) = 45 + 72 + 21 = 138 \] \[ EV_{\text{AI}} = 0.25(150) + 0.45(175) + 0.30(140) = 37.5 + 78.75 + 42 = 158.25 \] Balanced behavior (EV) recommends: AI–Driven network.

Maximin (safe behavior)

Worst payoff for each configuration: \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Centralized}, S_i) = \min\{210, 130, -40\} = -40 \] \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Regional}, S_i) = \min\{180, 160, 70\} = 70 \] \[ \min_i \text{Payoff}(\text{AI}, S_i) = \min\{150, 175, 140\} = 140 \] The safe behavior chooses the highest worst payoff: 140 (AI–Driven). Safe behavior recommends: AI–Driven network.

Maximax (risky behavior)

Best payoff for each configuration: \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Centralized}, S_i) = \max\{210, 130, -40\} = 210 \] \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{Regional}, S_i) = \max\{180, 160, 70\} = 180 \] \[ \max_i \text{Payoff}(\text{AI}, S_i) = \max\{150, 175, 140\} = 175 \] The risky behavior chooses the largest best payoff: 210 (Centralized). Risky behavior recommends: Centralized network.

Summary by behavior type

  • Safe behavior (Maximin): AI–Driven network.
  • Balanced behavior (Expected Value): AI–Driven network.
  • Risky behavior (Maximax): Centralized network.
